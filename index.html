ICTP-SAIFR Complex Systems & Statistical Mechanics Seminars

Welcome to ICTP-SAIFR Complex Systems & Statistical Mechanics Seminars webpage!

You will find all information about our seminar series in the different menus, how to register to receive notifications, and the list of previous and upcoming lectures.

ICTP-SAIFR Complex Systems & Statistical Mechanics Seminars is a biweekly digital seminar delivered via Zoom and targeting researchers and students interested in complex systems and statistical mechanics. We usually meet on Mondays at 14h30 BRT (UTC -3). Each seminar usually consists of a 45-50 minute presentation followed by time for discussion and questions.

Fill out our registration form to receive an email telling you how to connect to our next seminars!

These webinars are organized by Benjamin Garcia De Figueiredo, Raul Palma, Daniel Cardoso Pereira Jorge and João Valeriano, together with Professor Ricardo Martinez-García, and sponsored by ICTP/SAIFR. The ICTP South American Institute for Fundamental Research (ICTP-SAIFR) is a South American hub for theoretical physics created in 2011 as a collaboration of the International Centre for Theoretical Physics (ICTP) in Italy with the São Paulo State Research Funding Agency (FAPESP) and the São Paulo State University (UNESP). ICTP-SAIFR is located in the Instituto de Física Teorica (IFT-UNESP) in downtown São Paulo. Its goals are to conduct research in theoretical physics at the highest international standards, support research in South American regions where theoretical physics is less developed, and provide an international center for schools and workshops.

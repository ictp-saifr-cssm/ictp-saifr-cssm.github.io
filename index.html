ICTP-SAIFR Complex Systems Seminars

ICTP-SAIFR Complex Systems Seminars is a biweekly digital seminar on Zoom targeting researchers and students interested in complex systems. The seminars are about 1 h long followed by a discussion and are held on Mondays at 2:30 pm BRT, i.e, UTC -3. Fill out our registration form to receive an email telling you how to connect to our next seminars!

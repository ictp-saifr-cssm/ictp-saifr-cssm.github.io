ICTP-SAIFR Complex Systems & Statistical Mechanics Seminars

Welcome to ICTP-SAIFR Complex Systems & Statistical Mechanics Seminars!
In this page you will find all information about our seminar series, and also click on the top menu to see the upcoming and previous lectures.

ICTP-SAIFR Complex Systems & Statistical Mechanics Seminars is a biweekly digital seminar on Zoom targeting researchers and students interested in complex systems and statistical mechanics. The seminars are about 1 h long followed by a discussion and happen usually on Mondays at 14h30 BRT (UTC -3).

Fill out our registration form to receive an email telling you how to connect to our next seminars!

These webinars are organized by Benjamin Garcia De Figueiredo, Raul Palma and Vítor Sudbrack, together with Professor Ricardo Martinez-García, and sponsored by ICTP/SAIFR. Any doubts or suggestions, please, do not hesitate to contact us.

A lot of interesting content is coming next, and we can’t wait to share it with you all!
